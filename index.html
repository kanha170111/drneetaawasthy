About Me

A technology evangelist, an innovator, a design thinker, a speaker, a motivator. . With almost 40 research papers, three books, and two websites to my credit.

I am a graduate of HBTI, Kanpur with a Masters and PhD. in Electronics and Energy Auditor from the Bureau of Energy Efficiency from Govt. of India. Worked as a full time consultant for almost 10 years for turnkey computer solutions to mostly automobile industry, then became an academician. As a Professor and Director of Engineering College doing my duties to mentor the faculty, staff, and students for the last 10 years.
